Data Deletion & Account Removal

We respect your privacy. If you want your Yorkie Bakery account removed or would like us to delete any data associated with your Facebook login, please follow the steps below.

  • Send an email to yorkiebakery@gmail.com with the subject “Data Deletion Request”.
  • Include the email address you used to log in and a brief note that you’d like your data deleted.
  • We’ll confirm and remove your account data from our systems within 7 business days.

Note: Yorkie Bakery does not store your Facebook credentials. We only keep basic profile info needed for login and orders. If you have no orders, there may be no additional data to remove.
